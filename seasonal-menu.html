What's in season — ❄️ Winter · Jan – Mar
  • Hearty greens + cabbage: Swiss chard, kale, cavolo nero, Savoy cabbage — all peak winter. Wilt in coconut oil for breakfast skillet. Savoy Cabbage, Herby Sausage + Garlic Croutons is a standout feast menus dish.

Winter Breakfast Patties
Venison or bison · batch Saturday · makes 12 · freeze half
🔒 Daily anchorDairy-free
Ingredients 700 g ground venison or bison · ½ red onion, finely diced · 100 g mushrooms, finely chopped · 2 tbsp fresh parsley · 1 tbsp rosemary · ½ tsp crushed juniper berries · 1 tsp smoked paprika · 1 tsp sea salt · coconut oil to cook Method Combine all ingredients gently — don't overwork. Form into 12 patties (~80 g each), press a small indent in the centre. Cook in coconut oil over medium heat, 4–5 min per side until browned through. Cool completely, refrigerate week supply, freeze remainder individually.
Garlic, Thyme + Spring Onion Roast Chicken
1 × 1.6 kg whole chicken · Sunday roast · strips Mon–Wed · serves 4
🔒 Weekly anchor⚠️ Garlic
Ingredients 10 spring onions · 1½ tbsp olive oil · 1 × 1.6 kg chicken (out of fridge 15 min) · 1 lemon, halved · small bunch thyme · 1 tbsp sea salt flakes · ½ tsp cracked black pepper ⚠️ Garlic adaptation Replace the garlic bulb with garlic-infused EVOO brushed over the skin. Add extra thyme + lemon zest + a pinch of asafoetida inside cavity for depth. Roast at 200°C for 65 min. Rest 10 min minimum, then strip and portion into 3 days.

Mediterranean Salmon
1½ lbs wild salmon · 40 min · serves 4
Fish variationDairy-free
Ingredients 1 tbsp coconut oil · 1½ lbs wild-caught salmon · ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted and minced · ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped · ½ lemon, juiced · ¼ tsp sea salt · 3 tbsp EVOO Method Preheat 200°C. Spread coconut oil on a baking sheet. Combine olives, parsley, lemon, salt — spread over fillet. Bake 15 min or until fish flakes easily. Drizzle EVOO before serving.
Grilled Black Pepper + Lemon Mackerel
4 small whole mackerel · 10 min · serves 4
Fish backupDairy-free
Ingredients + method 2 lemons · 4 small mackerel (gutted, cleaned) · 2 tbsp olive oil · 1 tsp sea salt flakes · 2 tsp cracked black pepper. Preheat grill to high. Score fish 3× each side. Slice lemons thinly, stuff into cavity. Rub oil + salt + pepper inside and outside. Grill 5 min, flip, grill 5 more min until skin is crispy and charred and flesh is soft to touch.
Lamb Meatballs with Garlic + Spinach
Ground lamb · 30 min · serves 3–4 · dinner backup
Dinner backup⚠️ Garlic
Ingredients 6 kalamata olives, minced · 3 cloves garlic, minced → use garlic-infused oil · ½ tsp lemon zest · ½ tsp cinnamon · ½ tsp sea salt · 1 lb ground lamb · 2 tbsp coconut oil · ½ cup bone broth · 4 cups baby spinach · ½ lemon, juiced Method Combine olives, lemon zest, cinnamon, salt in bowl. Mix in lamb, form 1-inch meatballs. Sear in coconut oil over high heat until browned all sides. Add bone broth, cover, cook 5–6 min. Add spinach, stir until wilted. Drizzle lemon juice and serve.
🥚 Egg recipes — pre-workout + weekends
Golden Egg Skillet
4 eggs · 10–15 min · serves 2 · post-workout or weekend
Egg recipe⚠️ Garlic
Ingredients 2 tbsp avocado oil or ghee · 2 medium spring onions, white and green parts sliced · 1 clove garlic → use infused oil · 3.5 oz (100 g) Swiss chard or collard greens, stalks and leaves separated, sliced · 1 medium zucchini, sliced · 2 tbsp water · ½ tsp mustard · ½ tsp ground turmeric · ½ tsp black pepper · salt to taste · 4 large eggs · ¼ cup Manchego or Pecorino Romano (sheep's milk ✅) Method Preheat oven to 180°C fan / 200°C. Grease ovenproof skillet with oil. Cook spring onions and garlic-infused oil 1 min. Add chard stalks, zucchini, water. Stir in mustard, turmeric, pepper, salt until zucchini is just tender. Add chard leaves and cook until wilted. Make 4 wells, crack an egg into each. Top with cheese. Bake 5–7 min until whites are set.
Greek Breakfast Power Bowl
4 eggs · 20 min · serves 2 · weekend
Weekend breakfast⚠️ Garlic
Ingredients 3 tbsp avocado oil or ghee · 1 clove garlic → infused oil · 2 tsp fresh rosemary, chopped · 1 medium zucchini, roughly chopped · 1 medium (200 g) eggplant, roughly chopped · 3 tbsp lemon juice · 1½ tbsp fresh mint, chopped · salt and pepper · 70 g sheep's halloumi, sliced · ¼ cup kalamata olives · 4 large eggs, soft-boiled or poached · 1 tbsp EVOO to drizzle Method Heat skillet with oil. Add garlic-infused oil, zucchini, eggplant, cook 10–15 min until soft. Stir in lemon juice, mint, salt. Grease same pan medium-high, brown halloumi 2–3 min per side. Top veg with halloumi, olives, soft-boiled eggs. Drizzle EVOO. Serve warm.

🥗 Winter salads + sides
Emerald Kale Salad
15 min · serves 4 · salad base all week
Salad baseDairy-free
Ingredients 1 bunch curly-leafed kale, stemmed and finely chopped · 2 tbsp EVOO · 1 tsp sea salt · ½ lemon, juiced (1 tbsp) · ¼ cup minced red onion · ¼ cup finely chopped cucumber Method Place chopped kale in a large bowl. Drizzle with EVOO and sea salt. Massage gently with your hands for 5–10 minutes until the tough fibres break down and kale softens. Toss with red onion and cucumber. Keeps well in fridge for several days.
Blood Orange, Lentil + Fennel
15 min · serves 4 · lentils limited to ¼ cup per serving
Winter salad🔄 Carb-limited
Ingredients 80 g blanched hazelnuts · 1 large fennel bulb, sliced thinly · 2 blood oranges, peeled and sliced · 2 × 390 g fine green lentils (use ¼ cup per serving max — bulk with extra fennel and rocket) · small bunch dill · 1–2 lemons · 3 tbsp EVOO · ½–1 tsp sea salt · 100 g Manchego (sheep's milk ✅) · 65 g wild rocket · sourdough optional (skip) Method Roast hazelnuts 8–9 min at 200°C until golden. Slice top and bottom off oranges, remove pith. Slice into horizontal rounds. Wash fennel under cold water. Combine all. Squeeze juice of 1 lemon, drizzle oil, toss gently. Scatter Manchego shavings and hazelnuts over.
Radish + Jicama Tabbouli
15 min · serves 4 · grain-free
Side / saladGrain-free

In a small bowl whisk the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice and salt. Toss dressing with the salad and serve. Keeps in fridge for several days.
Rainbow Roasted Root Vegetables
1 hour · serves 4 · batch Saturday
Batch sideDairy-free
Ingredients 5 medium carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces · 3 medium beets, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes · 3 medium parsnips, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces · 1 small rutabaga, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces · 3 tbsp coconut oil, melted · ½ tsp sea salt Method Preheat oven 200°C. Combine all vegetables, coat evenly with melted coconut oil and salt. Transfer to baking dish. Bake about 1 hour, stirring every 20 min, until soft and browned on the outside. Serve warm. Sautéed Market Greens
20 min · serves 4
SideDairy-free
Ingredients 2 large bunches kale, chard, or collard greens, stemmed and roughly chopped · 2 tbsp coconut oil · 1 tsp sea salt Method Wash and dry greens thoroughly — not too wet before cooking. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat. When fat is melted and pan is hot, add greens in batches, stirring as they cook. It's fine if they don't all fit at once — keep adding as others cook. Add salt and cook for about 15 minutes, turning, until tender. Serve warm.
Warm Dill Lentils with Cumin Spinach
25 min · serves 4 · ¼ cup lentils per serving max
Hot side🔄 Carb-limited⚠️ Garlic
Ingredients 2 onions · 3 tbsp EVOO · ¼–½ tsp sea salt · 2 cloves garlic → use garlic-infused oil · 3 tsp ground cumin · large bunch dill, roughly chopped · 2 × 390 g tin green lentils (¼ cup per serving) · 1 lemon · 3 tbsp sheep's milk yoghurt or coconut yoghurt Method Peel and slice onions into half-moons, as finely as possible. Put oil in a large frying pan then cook onion with salt over low heat for 8 min. Add garlic-infused oil and cumin and cook for 5–6 more min. Rinse spinach in cold water, drain, squeeze out excess. Add dill to the onion along with the drained lentils, increase heat. Squeeze in juice of half the lemon. Cover and steam 1 min. Swirl yoghurt over to serve.

Caesar Salad Dressing
5 min · yields 1 cup · dairy-free
Dressing
¼ cup coconut concentrate (softened in warm water bath) · ¼ cup filtered water or coconut milk · ½ cup EVOO · 2 anchovy fillets · 1 lemon, juiced · ¾ tsp sea salt. Blend on high until smooth. If too thick, add water 1 tbsp at a time. Keeps in fridge several days — warm slightly before using as it hardens when chilled.

Classic Chicken Soup
Whole hen · 2 hrs · serves 6 · healing winter meal
Warming soup
One 4–5 lb stewing hen or rooster · 1 large onion, chopped · 1 bay leaf · 2 cloves garlic → infused oil ⚠️ · 1 tbsp sea salt + more to taste · 6 large carrots, chopped · 4 ribs celery, chopped · 2 lbs green beans, chopped. Begin by cleaning the chicken (rinse under cold water and remove loose bits). Place in a large stockpot. Fill the pot with cold water until chicken is just covered. Bring to a boil, then cover tightly and lower heat to a bare simmer. Cook until meat is tender and falling off bone, about 1–2 hours. Remove chicken, pour broth through strainer, saving broth. Add broth back with carrots, celery, green beans. Bring to boil then simmer about 20 min. Remove chicken meat and add back to soup. Salt to taste. Keeps in fridge several days, also freezes well.
Salmon Chowder
Salmon + vegetables · 2 hrs · serves 6
Warming soup
1 salmon carcass, bones, head and tail · 1 quart water · 1 bay leaf · 2 tbsp coconut oil · 1 large onion, chopped · 4 cloves garlic → infused oil ⚠️ · 4 parsnips, chopped (reduce by half to keep carbs lower) · 3 carrots, chopped · 2 ribs celery, chopped · 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar · 1½ tsp sea salt · 1 tsp dried thyme · 1½ lbs (680 g) salmon fillet, skin and bones removed, cut into 1½-inch chunks · 1 cup Creamy Coconut Milk · ½ lemon, juiced · 3 green onions, thinly sliced. Make broth: place salmon carcass in stockpot with water + bay leaf, simmer at lowest setting — essential to cook at lowest simmer possible. Remove carcass, strain broth. Cook onion in coconut oil until browned. Add garlic-infused oil and cook. Add parsnips, carrots, celery, thyme, salt, vinegar and broth. Bring to boil then simmer 30 min. Blend 2 cups without the carrots to make soup orange-ish. Add salmon chunks and coconut milk. Cook until piping hot (do not boil). Add lemon juice and serve garnished with green onions. Tastes best served fresh.

Roast Lemon, Dill + Spring Onion Trout
4 small whole trout · 20 min · serves 4
🔒 Weekly anchor
4 small whole trout, gutted and cleaned · 2 tbsp olive oil · 2 lemons · 8 spring onions · small bunch (30 g) dill · 1 tsp sea salt flakes. Preheat 200°C. Rub ½ tbsp oil over a roasting tray. Slice lemons into 5 mm rounds. Trim spring onions, cut 2 cm from green tops. Divide lemon slices, spring onions and dill between the fish cavities. Rub remaining oil over, scatter salt. Roast 20 min until skin is crisp and flesh is soft to touch.
Herb-Stuffed Trout
2 whole trout · 30 min · serves 2
Fish variation
1 tbsp coconut oil · 2 whole trout (~450 g each) · ½ tsp sea salt · 1 lemon, sliced into thin rounds · 4 large sprigs fresh rosemary · 4 large sprigs fresh thyme · kitchen twine. Preheat 230°C. Coat baking dish with fat. Sprinkle salt inside and outside fish. Fill cavity with lemon rounds and herb sprigs. Tie closed with twine to keep contents inside. Bake 15 min or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.

15 min · serves 4
Fish salad

Steak Salad Bowl with Asparagus, Avocado + Gremolata
Beef steak · 20 min · serves 2
Spring salad⚠️ Garlic
2 lean beef steaks (300 g) · 2 tbsp avocado oil · salt and pepper · 14–16 asparagus spears, trimmed and cut into 1½-inch pieces · 1 large avocado, sliced · 3 cups dark leafy greens (arugula, spinach, or watercress) · 1 small red onion, sliced · 2 tbsp lemon juice · fresh parsley, chopped · 2–3 cloves garlic → garlic-infused oil ⚠️ · grated lemon zest · 1 tbsp EVOO · Pecorino shavings (sheep's milk ✅, optional). How to think about your pantry
  • The pantry is what makes 10-minute assembly possible — if it's stocked, every meal is just protein + veg + dressing
  • Divide into three tiers: always stocked (never run out), rotate seasonally (swap every 3 months), and restock monthly (bulk items)
  • Buy EVOO in dark glass, cold-pressed, within 18 months of harvest — the "two-cough" test: a sip should make you cough twice from the oleocanthal

  • Dark chocolate: 85%+ minimum, high-flavanol — 28–57 g is the therapeutic dose
  • ⚠️ Garlic-infused oil: make your own (see Techniques) or buy pre-made, labelled oil only — not whole garlic
